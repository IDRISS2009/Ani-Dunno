Jujutsu Kaisen

3 October 2020 | +14 | Shonen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese anime and manga series by Gege Akutami. It follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who swallows a cursed object—a finger of the powerful Curse Ryomen Sukuna—and becomes its host. To control the curse, Yuji joins a school for Jujutsu sorcerers, where he learns to fight curses and stop dangerous spirits. The series blends intense action, supernatural elements, and dark themes, making it a standout in the shonen genre. The anime, released in 2020, is known for its stunning animation and epic fight scenes.

Powers and Techniques

Curse Technique

All Jujutsu sorcerers utilize curse techniques to combat cursed spirits. These techniques vary from person to person, allowing for unique fighting styles.

Shikigami Summoning

Megumi Fushiguro's technique allows him to summon shikigami, spiritual familiars that assist him in battle.

Cursed Speech

Toge Inumaki uses cursed speech, which enables him to manipulate others with his words.

Domain Expansion

Advanced sorcerers like Satoru Gojo can create a Domain Expansion, a space where their techniques are guaranteed to hit the target.

Blood Manipulation

Kamo Noritoshi can manipulate his blood and the blood of others, enhancing his combat abilities.

Cursed Tools

Nobara Kugisaki uses cursed tools infused with cursed energy, allowing her to perform powerful attacks on curses.

Physical Combat Skills

Maki Zenin excels in physical combat, using her strength and agility to defeat cursed spirits.

Episodes

Jujutsu Kaisen is an anime series that takes place over multiple seasons, each containing captivating episodes that explore the world of jujutsu sorcerers and curses.

Season 1

Number of episodes: 24

Air Date: October 2020 - March 2021

Main Arcs:

  • Introduction Arc (Episodes 1-5): Introduction of the main characters, the universe, and the curse of Sukuna.
  • Jujutsu Exam Arc (Episodes 6-13): Yuji and his friends participate in an exam that turns into a battle against curses.
  • Shibuya Arc (Episodes 14-24): Major confrontations with sorcerers and curses, highlighting character development and high stakes.

Season 2

Number of episodes: Upcoming (Scheduled for 2023)

Content: The season is expected to delve deeper into the manga arcs, particularly the backstory of Satoru Gojo and other important characters.

Films
